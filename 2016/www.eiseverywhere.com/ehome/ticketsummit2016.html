Join us for the 10th annual Ticket Summit July 12-14, 2016 at The Venetian Las Vegas!


Ticket Summit is the leading conference and trade show for ticketing and live entertainment executives and small business owners.

Ticket Summit brings together hundreds of industry leaders and small business owners for the ticketing and live entertainment industries for a one-of-a-kind networking event. From industry panel sessions and keynotes to a comprehensive trade show, Ticket Summit provides you with the industry tools and information you need to succeed!

Learn from Lemonis!


Ticket Summit welcomes Marcus Lemonis, host of CNBC's hit show, The Profit, as the keynote speaker at Ticket Summit 2016.

On Thursday, July 14, 2016 at 4 PM, Lemonis will give the keynote address, take questions from the audience, and take photos with attendees. Be there.
